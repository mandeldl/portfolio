About Me

I'm currently enrolled in the CodeCademy Labs in New York, bolstering my front-end knowledge with Ruby on Rails and AngularJS. I love writing front-end code on my own and with a dev team.

In my previous career as an independent film producer, I worked with and managed teams of 20 or more. I was responsible for the schedule, budget, and day-to-day operations of multi-million dollar projects. I love the immediacy of working with the web, and enjoy the fast-moving pace of our industry.

Before moving to New York 7 years ago, I lived in Washington, DC, Chicago, and all over the world. I lived in Moscow when the Soviet Union fell, but I'm not taking credit for that. Although I no longer make movies, I still enjoy watching them. My favorites are The Big Lebowski, The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou, and Die Hard.

Tools

I love coding in Sublime Text. As a longtime lifehacker, I enjoy the flexibility and the power of its commands and shortcuts. I use Emmet and a handful of other packages to quickly build semantic markup.

Frameworks

I relish every chance to create beautiful front-end experiences, but I'm also eager to challenge myself and become a full-stack developer. I'm currently learning Ruby on Rails and Angular JS.
