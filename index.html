About Me

I'm a web developer at Complex Media in New York, where I both code advertising units that run across a network of almost 100 sites and also manage a range of workflows that span both technical and non-technical teams.

In my spare time I enjoy exploring the latest front-end trends like ES2015 and frameworks like React and VueJS. I also enjoy playing around with full-stack frameworks built on Node and Ruby on Rails.

In my previous career as an independent film producer, I worked with and managed teams of 20 or more. I was responsible for the schedule, budget, and day-to-day operations of multi-million dollar projects. I love the immediacy of working with the web, and enjoy the fast-moving pace of our industry.

I've lived in NYC, Washington, DC, Chicago, and all over the world. I lived in Moscow when the Soviet Union fell, but I'm not taking credit for that. Although I no longer make movies, I still enjoy watching them. My favorites are The Big Lebowski, The Life Aquatic with Steve Zissou, and Die Hard.

Tools

I love coding in Sublime Text. As a longtime lifehacker, I enjoy the flexibility and the power of its commands and shortcuts. I use Emmet and a handful of other packages to quickly build semantic markup.

Frameworks
